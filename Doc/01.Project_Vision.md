# ResumePilot
## Vision
ResumePilot is an AI-powered SaaS platform that helps software engineers improve their resumes, analyze ATS compatibility, match resumes with job descriptions, and receive AI-driven career recommendations.
---

## Problem Statement
Many job seekers submit resumes without knowing:
- Whether their resume is ATS-friendly.
- Which skills are missing.
- Why they are not getting interviews.
- How well their resume matches a specific job.
---

## Our Solution
ResumePilot helps users by:
- Uploading resumes.
- Analyzing resumes using AI.
- Calculating ATS scores.
- Comparing resumes with job descriptions.
- Identifying missing skills.
- Suggesting resume improvements.
- Tracking resume analysis history.
---

## Target Audience
- Software Engineering Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Junior Developers
- MERN Stack Developers
- Frontend Developers
- Backend Developers
---

## Long-Term Vision
Become an AI-powered career assistant that helps developers build better resumes, prepare for interviews, and land better jobs.
